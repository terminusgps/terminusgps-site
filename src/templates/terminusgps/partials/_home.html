For Commerical Use

Today's fleet managers need a reliable and efficient way to keep up with today's demands on their fleet.

Whether you're keeping up with just a few service vehicles, or a large scale fleet operation with thousands of vehicles, we have the tools you need to properly manage your scaling fleet.

All vehicles in the fleet are accessible in one place but utilized in different ways by department, job or any other organizational structure your fleet has.

Here's what our commercial fleet managers cite as their most used capabilities:

  • Geographic location
  • Enter/exit of geofence areas
  • Time and distance traveled
  • Daily start and stop times
  • Driver behavior

For Individual Use

Whether you're monitoring a teen driver or an elderly driver, promoting safety and reducing driving risk is our first priority; it's the very idea Terminus GPS was founded on.

We've taken the industry standard GPS tracking software and hardware, mainly used in commerical fleet tracking, and simplified it for individual users.

Alerts you create and configure in the platform are sent via SMS directly to your smartphone.

Here's what our individual users cite as their most used capabilities:

  • Geographic location
  • Accelerated speed
  • Time and distance traveled
  • Stop and start times
  • Driver behavior monitoring

Don't want text messages?

You can access the exact same notification data by logging into the platform from any Internet connected device.
