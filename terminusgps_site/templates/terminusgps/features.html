Terminus GPS Features

Finally, a GPS tracking solution for everyone.

One Platform To Digitalize Them All

From trucks and trailers to drivers and passengers, every individual part of your fleet is conveniently digitalized.

Terminus GPS illuminates every part of your fleet in a simple, easy-to-use, always online interface.

Can't access a desktop computer? The Terminus GPS platform is also available as native iOS and Android applications.

Live Alerts Delivered Directly To Your Device

Maintenance coming due? Refueling needed before the next leg? Engine temperature running too high?

With Terminus GPS, your mobile device becomes your lifeline to your fleet.

Always stay up-to-date with live sms/text-to-speech voice notifications, configured specifically for your fleets' needs.

Fuel Management

You shouldn't be in the dark when it comes to your fleets' fuel levels.

Every vehicle's fuel level is constantly tracked and logged, providing you with vision deep inside your operations.

Stay on top of fuel requirements and monitor potential fuel theft to keep your business operating smoothly.

Geofencing

The Terminus GPS platform conforms to your needs, not the other way around.

Draw custom geofence zones in the platform, areas of Earth's surface you configure in our convenient web interface.

Get informed when a vehicle enters a jobsite, the office or the shop, all from your mobile device or desktop computer.

Route Optimization

Define routes in the Terminus GPS platform, scheduled rides with configured checkpoints.

Top-of-the-line algorithms automatically optimize your routes so you can focus on running your business.

Generate reports, optimize for fuel efficiency and monitor progress without missing a beat.
