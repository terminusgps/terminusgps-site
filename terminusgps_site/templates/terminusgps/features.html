What Do You Get?

A GPS tracking solution for everyone.

One Platform to Digitalize Them All

Terminus GPS illuminates every part of your fleet in a simple, easy-to-use, always online interface. From trucks and trailers to drivers and passengers, every individual part of your fleet is conveniently digitalized.

Can't access a desktop computer? Terminus GPS is also available as native iOS and Android applications.

Live Alerts Delivered Directly to Your Phone

Maintenance coming due? Refueling needed before the next leg? Engine temperature running way too high?

Terminus GPS turns your mobile device into an always-on lifeline to your fleet. Stay up-to-date with live sms or text-to-speech voice notifications configured specifically for your fleets' needs.

Fuel Micro, Managed

You shouldn't be in the dark when it comes to your fleets' fuel levels. Every vehicle's fuel level is constantly tracked and logged, illuminating all aspects of your fleet's fuel management.

Stay on top of fuel requirements and mitigate fuel theft, keeping your business operating smoothly and efficiently without lifting a finger.

Still on the Geofence?

Need to know exactly when a vehicle enters a jobsite, the office or the shop?

Draw custom geofence zones in the platform, areas of Earth's surface you configure in our convenient web interface, and get informed instantly when a vehicle enters or exits that location.

Optimal Routing With One Click

Terminus GPS algorithmically optimizes your fleets' routes so you can focus on actually running your business.

Define routes in the Terminus GPS platform, scheduled rides with configured checkpoints. Once created, you may generate reports, optimize for fuel efficiency and monitor route progress of those routes in the same, convenient interface.
